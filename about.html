About Our Organization

Committed to building peace and improving lives in our communities

Who We Are

Dandi Concerned Citizen Initiatives (CCI) is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of underserved communities. We focus on education, healthcare, social support, peace advocacy, and community development.

We also promote security awareness through sensitization programs and collaboration with local security groups and agencies. Our goal is to help maintain peace, reduce conflict, and build safer communities.

Our Mission

To provide support, opportunities, and essential resources to vulnerable people while promoting peace, security, and sustainable development.

Our Vision

A peaceful and empowered society where everyone has access to basic needs, education, safety, and opportunities to thrive.
